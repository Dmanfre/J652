Student loans issues continue to rise in NJ, MD

Story and data viz by Dylan Manfre

Navient is a large provider of student loans in the United States of America. It reached a settlement on Jan. 13, according to an article from Student Loan Planner.

Residents from New Jersey and Maryland filed many complaints about student loans. Over 600 of those complaints from both states regarded issues with the loan lender or servicer. In New Jersey, complaints increased over a three year period.
