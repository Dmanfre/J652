The Modernization of Basketball

Basketball has changed incredibly since it was first invented in the late 1800s and the game itself has been constantly evolving from LeBron James' emphatic dunks and Steph Curry's smooth 3-pointers.

Another thing that has changed has been the structure of the basketball hoop. It was first just a wicker basket with no backboard. Then, to mount it to a wall, the backboard was introduced. This evolved into a free-standing basket whcih then became adjustable. Now, basketball hoops are made of medal rims and glass backboards. They're so advanced you can even mount a camera on top of it.
